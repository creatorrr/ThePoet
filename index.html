Of this violently beautiful Universe.

Its endless chaos.


I am surrounded by extraordinary beauty.

A flurry of emotions.

The teeming millions.

Living.

Breathing the same air.


Each last particle dancing,

Jiggling to the one rhythm,

Reverberating through the Cosmos.

What would I not give,

To live like this forever...

~ When I Meditate ~

As I close my eyes

A million voices begin to sing

An eternal hymn

A ballad of the Wisdom of the ages


The sound of breath

The music of silence

Echoes through my heart

Invigorates the soul

Fleeting flashes

Of Colours old and new

The aroma of love

The moisture on her lips


A surge of passion

The unrelenting nostalgia

Agony and regret

Burn within the bosom

Then it all fades away

Visions subside

Sounds muffle

A gripping calm ensues


Beyond all pain

Beyond all pleasure

Standing on the brink of existence

I pass into nothingness

Finally.
I fell in love.
All over again..

Sigh.

I have fallen in love.

I have found the paradox, that if you love until it hurts, there can be no more hurt, only more love.

~ I have fallen in love. ~

I have fallen in love.

With this wonderful world.

With this beautiful life.


Look around.

Everything is just so beautiful.

It gives me so much happiness to live in the moment.

To relish the sheer joy

of learning something new everyday.

The passion of work.

The tranquility of solitude.


Think about it.

Let's just chuck all our worries in a closet.

And lock it forever.

Let's learn how to live.

Not just persist through our day.

Have the courage to do what you love.


Those little things you never care about.

Matter a lot.

Pick up the phone.

Tell people that you love them.

Say sorry.

Be grateful.


You ain't got as much time as you think sonny.

Laugh a little more.

Look at a mirror and smile.

Know that no one can take that away.

I'm only surprised why I've never done this before.


Now, I smile. I dance. I cry.

I can't waste even a single moment of my life in regret.

Anguish and pain are part of life.

But, happiness too is an equal part.

Accept them.


Learn from the moments of sorrow.

Then, laugh them off.


Work. Hard.

Play. Harder.

Take part in a marathon.

Run till your lungs burn.

Relish the moment of victory.


Whatever you do.

Listen to your heart.

Don't let obsessions steal the fun.

Always know when to take a break.

When to get on the table and dance.


Just remember.

Only one thing matters.

And that's you.

Got drunk one day.
Too much grape juice gets to your head.

Then, I discovered Urdu.

Voices.
